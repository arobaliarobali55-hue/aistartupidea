📄 Product Requirement Document (PRD)
Product Name: TemOS
1️⃣ Product Overview

TemOS is an AI-powered SaaS web application that helps users discover personalized business ideas by answering structured questions.

The system analyzes 20+ personal data points and generates tailored business ideas displayed in swipeable card format.

2️⃣ Problem Statement

Many aspiring entrepreneurs struggle to:

Find a realistic business idea

Match ideas with their budget & skills

Understand where to start

Avoid information overload

TemOS solves this by generating focused, personalized suggestions in under 2 minutes.

3️⃣ Target Audience

Primary Users:

Students (16–25 years old)

Aspiring entrepreneurs

Side hustle seekers

Freelancers looking for new opportunities

Secondary Users:

Working professionals wanting a second income

Beginners confused about business direction
